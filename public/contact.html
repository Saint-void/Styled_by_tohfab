Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we offer custom design services for special occasions, bridal wear, and personalized pieces. Please contact us to schedule a consultation with our designer to discuss your vision and requirements.

How can I schedule a fitting appointment?

You can schedule a fitting appointment by calling our studio at +1 (555) 123-4567 or by filling out the contact form on this page. Please provide your preferred date and time, and we'll confirm your appointment based on availability.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unworn items in their original condition with tags attached. Custom-made pieces are non-returnable. Please contact our customer service team for more information about our return process.

Do you offer alterations for purchased items?

Yes, we offer alterations for all purchased items to ensure a perfect fit. Basic alterations are complimentary, while more extensive modifications may incur additional charges. Please contact us to schedule an alteration appointment.
