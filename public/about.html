About STYLED-BY-TOHFAB

Discover the story, philosophy, and people behind our fashion brand

Oduloye Hadijat Bolajoko

Founder & Creative Director

With over 15 years of experience in the fashion industry, Oduloye Hadijat Bolajoko has established herself as a visionary designer known for her elegant and timeless creations.

After graduating from the prestigious Fashion Institute of Design, she worked with several renowned fashion houses before launching her own label, STYLED-BY-TOHFAB, in 2010.

Her designs have been featured in Vogue, Elle, and Harper's Bazaar, and have been worn by celebrities on red carpets around the world.

Our Philosophy

"Fashion is not just about creating beautiful clothes; it's about empowering women to feel confident, elegant, and true to themselves. Every piece I design is a celebration of femininity and individuality."

At STYLED-BY-TOHFAB, we believe in sustainable fashion that stands the test of time. Our designs combine classic elegance with contemporary elements, creating pieces that are both timeless and modern.

We are committed to ethical production practices and use only the finest materials sourced from responsible suppliers. Each garment is meticulously crafted with attention to detail, ensuring exceptional quality and fit.

Our Journey

The evolution of TOHFAB from a small studio to an internationally recognized fashion brand

2010

The Beginning

TOHFAB was founded in a small studio in New York City with a collection of just 12 pieces.

2013

First Flagship Store

Opened our first flagship store in SoHo, New York, showcasing our expanding collection.

2015

International Expansion

Expanded to international markets with boutiques in Paris and London.

2018

Sustainable Initiative

Launched our sustainable fashion initiative, committing to eco-friendly practices.

2020

Digital Transformation

Enhanced our digital presence to connect with customers worldwide.

2023

Today

Continuing to innovate and inspire with our designs, reaching fashion enthusiasts globally.
